To access the underlying data of the dashboard, you'll also need to assign users with `Dashboard creator`, `Dashboard admin`, or `Dashboard editor` roles to the [`Analytics Viewer` pre-built team](/konnect-platform/teams-and-roles/#predefined-teams).
      
{:.info}
> **Note:** We are actively working on future improvements that will remove the requirement for most users to be assigned to pre-built teams to access analytics data. Look for these enhancements in upcoming releases.
