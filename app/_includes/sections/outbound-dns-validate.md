Once your outbound DNS resolver configuration displays as ready, you can begin using your Dedicated Cloud Gateway. 
To verify that it's ready, do the following:

1. In the {{site.konnect_short_name}} sidebar, click **API Gateways**.
1. Click your {{include.provider}} Dedicated Cloud Gateway.
1. In the API Gateways sidebar, click **Networks**.
1. From the action menu next to your {{include.provider}} network, select "Configure private DNS".
1. Scroll until you see `Ready` for your outbound DNS resolver.
